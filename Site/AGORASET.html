AGORASET: a dataset for crowd video analysis


Pierre Allain, Nicolas Courty, Thomas Corpetti, Clément Creusot

 

OVERVIEW


Agoraset is a dataset dedicated to researchers working on crowd video analysis.

It is composed of eight scenes where for each one, we give


  1. -Videos corresponding to various situations (viewing angle, lighting conditions, stress of the crowd, ...)

  2. -Position of pedestrians, associated velocity

  3. -The matlab readers

  4. -A set of matlab visualization tools (evolution of the eulerian density, ...)


In addition, segmentation images with single colors for each pedestrians are available Details.
